"DO OR DO NOT, THERE IS NO TRY", Master Yoda instructs a clouded young Luke Skywalker when he attempts to lift his X-wing fighter from the swamp, a philosophy that has guided me through my academic and professional journey, driving my passion and commitment all along.

As an imminent graduate with a Master of Science degree in Information Systems from Northeastern University, I am continually honing my knowledge and expertise in data science, with a primary focus on data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and AI. Proficient in Python and SQL, my role as a Junior Data Scientist at HarbourVest Partners involved conducting elaborate business and data analysis, helping me understand the profound impact of data on management-level decision-making processes. I have also engineered predictive models for most of my projects using a variety of algorithms and interesting use cases. Transforming complex data into actionable insights through sophisticated statistical strategies is something I am always comfortable doing and have proven to be good at.

Driven by this desire to tackle complex challenges, I would love to leverage my expertise to further enhance corporate performance, all while continuously adapting to the evolving nuances of the data science domain. In all essence, I live by Yoda's words, embracing challenges head-on and striving to excel in this ever-dynamic world of data and science.

Aside from this, cinema serves as another beating heart for me. It's often the only thing I ponder over and write about, sharing my thoughts, reviews, and analyses on my Letterboxd and Instagram pages. This passion for cinema hugely influences and enhances my capability to perform detailed data analysis, as I love getting under the skin of films and thus uncovering their essence, much like I enjoy doing with data.
